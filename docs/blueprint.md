# **App Name**: EDUPEAK

## Core Features:

- Offline Learning Platform: Provide a Progressive Web App (PWA) delivered via a proprietary, offline-first local network, ensuring continuous access to educational content, quizzes, and progress tracking, even without internet connectivity. Content delivered from a local micro-server such as a Raspberry Pi.
- Gamified Student Dashboard: Display a student dashboard with a progress wheel, total points/XP, and easy access to skill quests, designed to drive engagement and sustained motivation through gamification.
- AI-Powered Localization Tool: Utilize an embedded AI model to contextualize curriculum text with Nigerian geography, local markets, and cultural references. The tool will incorporate the user's chosen language setting if specified, when preparing material.
- Multi-Language Support: Incorporate a clear toggle/menu for Hausa, Igbo, and Yoruba languages to ensure core learning concepts, instructions, and navigation can be switched to promote literacy and deeper understanding in the student's native language.
- Teacher Analytics Dashboard: Provide teachers with a dashboard displaying aggregated student skill mastery scores, completion rates, and areas of collective difficulty, enabling data-driven instruction.
- Offline Teacher Professional Development Hub: Offer pre-loaded, self-paced training modules for teachers on modern pedagogy, digital tool integration, and subject-specific content mastery, accessible without internet connectivity, with a certification tracker.
- Infrastructure and Accountability Digital Twin (IADT): Implement a system where admins and teachers can input information on key school facilities with features such as Facility Health Scorecards, Maintenance Request & Tracking and Asset Utilization Logs

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep teal (#008080) to evoke trustworthiness and intelligence.
- Background color: Light teal (#E0F8F7), a desaturated variant of the primary, to maintain a cohesive visual experience.
- Accent color: Muted gold (#B8860B), analogous to teal, used to highlight key interactive elements and notifications.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif typeface providing a modern, objective and neutral feel, is used for all text to maximize legibility on various screen sizes.
- Use clean, simple icons with sufficient contrast against the background to ensure usability.
- Maintain a consistent layout across all portals and modules to minimize cognitive load and ensure ease of navigation.
- Implement subtle animations and transitions to provide feedback and enhance user experience without being distracting.
